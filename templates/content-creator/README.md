# Content Creator Team

Research, script, SEO, and review pipeline for content creators.

## Who is this for?

YouTubers, bloggers, newsletter writers, and social media creators who want an AI team to handle the full content pipeline — from research to final review.

## Agent Team

| Role | Model | Responsibility |
|------|-------|---------------|
| Researcher | claude-haiku-4-5 | Find trending angles, gather facts, identify compelling hooks |
| Scriptwriter | claude-sonnet-4-6 | Transform research into engaging scripts/articles matched to platform tone |
| SEO Specialist | claude-haiku-4-5 | Optimize titles, descriptions, tags, keywords, and posting strategy |
| QA Reviewer | claude-haiku-4-5 | Review for accuracy, engagement, platform fit, and strong CTAs |

## Example Goal

```
Create YouTube video script about "AI tools for small businesses" for YouTube
```

## Estimated Cost

~$0.07 per run

## Tips for Best Results

- Be specific about your platform — tone varies between YouTube, Twitter, newsletters
- Include your target audience in the goal for better-tailored content
- The SEO agent activates when keywords like "seo", "optimize", or "tags" appear in the goal
- Run multiple iterations to refine — the reviewer catches issues the writer misses
