# Research & Intelligence Team

Market research, competitor analysis, and synthesis pipeline.

## Who is this for?

Product managers, founders, and analysts who need structured market intelligence — competitor landscapes, trend analysis, and actionable recommendations.

## Agent Team

| Role | Model | Responsibility |
|------|-------|---------------|
| Market Researcher | claude-haiku-4-5 | Find primary sources, identify trends, surface non-obvious insights |
| Competitive Analyst | claude-haiku-4-5 | Map competitor strengths/weaknesses, find positioning gaps |
| Strategic Analyst | claude-sonnet-4-6 | Synthesize findings into clear insights with 3 concrete recommendations |
| Report Author | claude-sonnet-4-6 | Structure intelligence into skimmable reports with executive summaries |

## Example Goal

```
Research AI code assistant market with focus on developer adoption trends
```

## Estimated Cost

~$0.06 per run (recommended minimum 3 runs for depth)

## Tips for Best Results

- Use specific angles to focus the research (e.g., "pricing trends" vs just "market")
- The competitive analyst activates when keywords like "competitor" or "landscape" appear
- Run 3+ iterations — each pass deepens the analysis
- The synthesizer always ends with 3 actionable recommendations
